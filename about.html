About Hangman

The Hangman game is a classic word guessing game where you need to find the hidden word by guessing one letter at a time. If you guess incorrectly too many times, you lose!

How to Play

Press a letter key on your keyboard to guess a letter. If the letter is in the word, it will appear in its correct position. If the letter is not in the word, a part of the hangman will be drawn. Try to guess the word before the hangman is completely drawn!

Game Rules

The Hangman game has a set of rules that you should follow to play effectively.

Scoring System

Your score is determined based on how quickly you guess the word and the number of incorrect guesses.
